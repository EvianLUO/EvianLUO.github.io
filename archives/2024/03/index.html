About me

Hello and welcome to my profile!

I am Yiyun LUO, a dedicated Ph.D. candidate cultivated jointly by City University of Hong Kong (supervised by Yanto Chandra) and Zhejiang University (supervised by Aiqi WU). With a deep passion for academic research, my academic journey is centered in the field of entrepreneurship and strategic management.

My research is surround digital platforms, entrepreneurial framing, optimal distinctiveness. Alongside my studies, I have gained pratical experience in Tencent (Top internet company in China), which has enriched my understanding of China business environment.

In addition to my research, I actively engage with the academic community of AOM, AIB and SMS. I am always eager to learn and stay abreast of the latest developments in entrepreneurship research.

Networking and collaboration are the keystones of academic growth, and I am open to connecting with fellow researchers, academics, and professionals who share my interest in entrepreneurship. Whether you have questions about my research, are interested in discussing potential collaborations, or simply wish to exchange ideas, please feel free to reach out.

Thank you for visiting my profile. Looking forward to the opportunities to connect!
